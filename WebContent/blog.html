Vegetarians May Live Longer
June 1, 2016

Vegetarians may live longer than meat-lovers, new research suggests. Scientists in California analyzed the diets of 73,300 Seventh Day Adventists, and found that vegetarians were less likely to die from any cause or from cause-specific reasons, except for cancer, compared to those who ate meat.
