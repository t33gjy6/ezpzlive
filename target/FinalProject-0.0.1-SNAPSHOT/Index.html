Changing Your Diet One Meal At A Time

This site is designed to make your move from a carnivores diet to a vegetarian diet as easy as possible.

The way we are doing it is by getting your favorite meals and giving you a healthy vegetarian substitute that tastes similar.

That way you are still able to eat your favorite food and not miss the flavor that you crave.

It is Easy This is how it works

First choose your protein, it can be chicken, beef, pork or fish.

Next pick the meal that you want.
